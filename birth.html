February 9th, 2002

In the cool hospital room stand the family surrounding the newborn baby girl.

Mom: “Oh honey, look how beautiful she is!”

Aunt: “We’ve got ourselves a future heartbreaker!”,
marveled the aunt.

Dad: “Have you decided on a name, darling?”

Mom: “I finally know what we’re settling with.”

Music booming and chatter filled the atmosphere in household.
The yard was occupied by the little kids playing tag and popping
balloons. Under the apple tree sat the birthday girl and her favorite
cousins giggling and playing with the latest Bratz dolls.Wiping her
hands on her dress, Lexi finally placed her doll next to the
other dolls in the small trench she dug up.

“Okay girls, we can have a pool party now,” her cousin spoke in a
high pitched voice as she finished puttingon the swimsuit on her doll.

“Lexi, it’s time to open the presents!” Mother came over
and lifted Lexi up. She gasped and furiously
wiped off the now smudged mud stains on her sparkly dress.
“What did I say about playing in the mud, Lexi?”

Ripping open her third gift now, she smiled slightly as she pulled
out a battery operated baby doll, her first and second being a
doctor set and a giant dollhouse. The cameras kept clicking as she
pulled out another box. It was a nintendo. Before saying anything,
Mother interrupted by bringing out the cake.

Not knowing what to play with, she narrowed it down to her doctor set and nintendo.

"You would be a great doctor one day Lexi. You have
to study lots and lots but you will be the best! "

Mom comes in and closes the game and insists on playing with the other toys.
