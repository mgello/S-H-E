Sex-ism [seks-izm]
//noun//
Sexism is prejudice or discrimination based on a
person's sex or gender.



Who comes to mind when you see or hear the word sexist?
Women and girls have always been a target.



SHE was created to show how sexism still exists, where it starts and how it affects the protagonist growing up and her environment.

The story is also developed from the teams experiences altogether and fit to one story.
'Treat others the way you want to be treated.'


Everyone should be treated the same way, regardless of their gender, orientation, or looks.
